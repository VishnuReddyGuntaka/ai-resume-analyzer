🤖 AI Resume Analyzer

An advanced AI-powered resume analyzer built with **HTML, CSS, JavaScript**, and the **Claude AI API**.

Paste your resume → get an instant ATS score, job match %, suggestions, and missing keywords.

## ✨ Features

- **ATS Score** — checks if your resume passes Applicant Tracking Systems
- **Overall Score** — rates your resume quality out of 100
- **Job Match %** — how well your resume fits the target role
- **Strengths & Weaknesses** — specific feedback on your resume
- **Missing Keywords** — keywords recruiters are looking for
- **Top 5 Suggestions** — actionable improvements
- **Skills to Add** — in-demand skills you're missing

## 💡 How It Works

1. User pastes resume text
2. Optionally pastes job description
3. Selects target role
4. Claude AI analyzes the resume and returns:
   - Scores (ATS, Overall, Job Match)
   - Strengths and weaknesses
   - Missing keywords
   - Personalized suggestions
